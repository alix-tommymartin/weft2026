Basic information

The WEFT 2026 (Women’s European Football Tournament) is the only student women’s football tournament in Europe. This year's edition is scheduled on April 10–11, 2026, on the HEC Paris campus. It will bring together 8 European teams for a sportive and festive weekend.

Who can register ?

We are looking for student female teams who know how to play football with 8 people, whatever their level is. It's the perfect occasion to spend a teambuilding week-end close to Paris to discover the capital. Don't hesitate to register !

About the tournament

The event pursues a dual ambition: to promote a sport that is both more inclusive and more sustainable. On the social side, the tournament will feature an n&Out stand raising awareness about inclusion and mental health, a “Women in Business & Sport” roundtable with inspiring women from the sports world, and an inter-promotion gala match bringing together former and current FHF players. Environmentally, the WEFT will implement an eco-differentiated pricing system, distribute sustainable welcome kits, and provide local seasonal meals with vegetarian options. With a zero-waste goal and 100% of participants housed on campus to prevent transport emissions, WEFT 2026 embodies the commitment of a new generation to a female, united, and responsible sport.

WEFT package

Each team may travel with a maximum of 12 players and 1 coach. If you want to invite other people to support, they will have to self-organise their stay as they are not included in accomation, meals... In line with our CSR commitment, we have implemented differentiated membership fees based on the type of transport used. We are proud to say that despite inflation, we have managed to maintain last year's fees !

  • Approximately €80/person for teams using sustainable transport (train, bus)
  • Approximately €105/person for teams flying
    This fee includes:
  • 2 nights of accommodation (Friday and Saturday)
  • 4 meals (from Friday dinner to Saturday dinner)
  • A kit of useful and eco-friendly goodies and
  • Two unforgettable parties
  • Access to facilities, referees, and first aid station
    This fee does not include:
  • The cost of round-trip transportation
  • Lunch on Friday and Sunday

Schedule

Please plan to arrive between 11am and 1pm on Friday since the matches will begin at 2pm. You are welcome to stay until 12pm on Sunday on campus but we highly recommend spending your Sunday in Paris !! The detailed schedule will be shared once all teams have completed their registration. In the meantime, don’t hesitate to reach out — whether you have questions about logistics, accommodation, or the tournament itself, our team will be more than happy to help. We’ll make sure you have all the information you need to prepare for an unforgettable WEFT experience! ⚽
